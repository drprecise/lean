Normalization fields:
  pair.from, pair.to -> symbol
  pair.groupIndex -> group (category, leverage range)
  pair.feeIndex -> fee schedule (open/close fees)
  pair.spreadP -> spread percentage
  group.maxLeverage, group.minLeverage -> leverage bounds
  fee.openFeeP, fee.closeFeeP -> trade fees

Execution input contract:
  Trading requires wallet connection (on-chain: Arbitrum, Polygon, Base)
  Open position: pairIndex, long/short, leverage, positionSizeDai, tp, sl
  Close position: pairIndex, index, tradeIndex
  Position view: requires trader address
